How it Works: 

                There is a secret number between 1 to 6. You have to guess that number.
                If your guess is perfect, You will get 2 Points. If the difference
                is 1, You will get 1 Point. If the difference is 2 or more,
                You won't get any points. GOOD LUCK!!

                NB: 
                    - The secret number changes on every guess.
                    - After each 5 guess, you will be notified.
